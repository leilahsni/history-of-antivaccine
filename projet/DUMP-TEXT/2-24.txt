   and the selection of presidential candidates

   The people are suffering and hungry, so we need to put 100 trillion in
   the pot for people's livelihood and cook food in a hurry...

   - Columnist Lee Rae-gwon


   Since the Black Death of the Middle Ages and the modern Spanish Flu,
   mankind has been given a bonus by the global economic downturn and the
   polarization of wealth in the unprecedented epidemic of Corona 19, and
   each country is terrified. The process of understanding and overcoming
   this problem is the same, but broadly speaking, China is a superpower
   that blocks the entire city, risking a drop in economic growth rate,
   and controlling the population of 1.3 billion with socialist media
   control and penalties. The U.S. mandated vaccine boosters mainly for
   public institutions, saying, "Covid-19 is not something that can be
   overcome in the short term, so let's be friends." Korea is facing the
   fatigue and collapse of the medical system that has to rush social
   distancing again, so it made it compulsory for the 500,000 high school
   students or younger to enter private training facilities and private
   academies and only those who check the QR code, but the mother's unit
   was fiercely resisted. It is pitiful that people are wasting time
   looking at the sluggishness of the government and only looking at the
   opinions of experts on policy adjustment.

   Despite the fear of Omicron, the U.S. has a lower vaccination rate than
   Korea, despite being the largest producer of corona vaccines, with the

   in San Francisco with the threat of multi-gun robbery, robbing the
   checkout counter, breaking into mart bikes, knocking down the elderly
   on the street, robbing their wallets and running away. An acquaintance
   with 26 years of acquaintance reported that it had entered the state of
   the eve of a riot, where only people were shopping. Americans are more
   afraid of the aftereffects that they will have to live with for the
   rest of their lives than a full recovery from the coronavirus, and even
   the $1 trillion in economic stimulus from the Biden administration is
   nothing more than money to be invested in professionals, the middle
   class, and the state-led SOC. It is said that it is a desperate
   situation in which it is necessary to survive even by stealing.

   In China, the central government controls the number of cases and
   deaths from the media and takes care of the socially underprivileged
   based on the price of 500 won per meal in a socialist way. I tend to
   conform to communist propaganda. As public officials and military
   police provide daily necessities to quarantined areas free of charge,
   the people obey, and the state emphasizes the superiority of the system
   day after day and propagates those scenes through the state-run media.
   It is successful as a controlled society.

   We benchmarked in the American way, but there are 7,000 cases a day and
   70 deaths per day. It seems that there are indeed many problems as only

   in order to verify the ability to implement a clear policy before the
   presidential election.

   The United States, China, and Japan are key currency countries, so if
   the country collapses, the alliance will fail as well. Reserve
   countries can adjust interest rates to control inflation, but we do not
   have international acceptance of such authority and capacity. In such
   an unfavorable circumstance, they made good progress externally with
   the example of K-Quarantine and a booming export economy. The
   responsibility to appease the angry public by distributing the fruits
   of growth in each sector where the state and entrepreneurs become rich
   and the common people fall into hell and become debtors will be a
   prerequisite for the next leader.

   There is nothing more precious than life. There are no hunger people,
